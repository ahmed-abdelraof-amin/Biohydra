About BioHydra

BioHydra is an innovative agri-biotechnology startup pioneering the transformation of industrial starch and glucose waste into sustainable hydrogel solutions for agriculture.

Our Mission

To revolutionize water management in agriculture through biodegradable hydrogel technology, reducing water consumption while increasing crop yields.

Our Vision

A world where sustainable agriculture thrives even in arid regions, powered by circular economy solutions that transform industrial waste into agricultural value.

The Agricultural Water Crisis

Addressing critical challenges in modern farming

Water Scarcity

Agriculture accounts for 70% of global freshwater withdrawals, with increasing scarcity in arid regions.

Moisture Loss

Up to 60% irrigation water is lost to evaporation and deep percolation in conventional farming.

Soil Degradation

Poor water retention leads to soil erosion, nutrient loss, and decreased agricultural productivity.

BioHydra Hydrogel - The Future of Smart Water Management

BioHydra Hydrogel - Water retention capacity: 400x

How BioHydra Works

Our hydrogel acts as a microscopic water reservoir in the soil, absorbing and retaining up to 400 times its weight in water, then releasing it gradually to plant roots.

  • 100% Biodegradable
  • Non-toxic & Eco-friendly
  • Lasts 3-5 growing seasons
  • Compatible with all soil types
